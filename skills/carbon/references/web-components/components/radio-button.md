## Overview

Radio buttons represent a group of mutually exclusive choices, compared to
checkboxes that allow users to make one or more selections from a group. In use
cases where only one selection from a group is allowed, use the radio button
component instead of the checkbox. Though the `cds-radio-button` components may
be rendered individually, it is recommended they be rendered as children
components of the `cds-radio-button-group` parent component to maintain their
proper controlled states.
